# Smarter Interview Analyzer

![Dashboard Screenshot](images/dashboard.png)

An **AI-powered web application** to analyze resumes and interview answers, giving actionable insights to improve your interview readiness.

---

## **Features**

- 📄 **Upload Resume**: Get instant scoring and feedback on structure and content.
- 🎤 **Answer Analysis**: Type or speak your interview answers and receive AI feedback on **clarity, confidence, and relevance**.
- 📊 **Resume vs Answer Match**: See how closely your answers align with your resume.
- 📝 **Download Report**: Export AI-generated analysis in a PDF report.
- 👤 **Profile Management**: Add personal details and profile photo.
- 🗑 **Delete Resume**: Easily remove your uploaded resume and upload a new one.

---

## **Tech Stack**

- **Backend**: Python 3, Flask
- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **AI Analysis**: TF-IDF similarity (scikit-learn)
- **PDF Handling**: PyPDF2, FPDF
